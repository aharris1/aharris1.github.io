Thermoelectric Collector Plate

Rapid temperature control through the Peltier effect.

Project Successes

  • Above-ambient temperature settings could be maintained within 2°C.
  • Temperature could be driven bidirectionally.
  • Sub-ambient temperatures below 0°C were achievable.
  • Project proposal was accepted as finalist in American Society of Mechanical Engineers 2016 Student Manufacturing Design Competition

Project Failures

  • Temperature oscillations were not eliminated.
  • Solid-state relays failed before tuning of control system was complete.
  • System was not completed in time to be integrated into full system.

Lessons Learned and Skills Developed

  • Control systems - Asymmetric PID control using time-proportional control was initially implemented, and later augmented with gain-scheduling to account for varying actuator power over the operating temperatures.
  • Electronic component familiarity
    • Relays - Electromechanical relays were initially selected including SPST and DPDT relays. The delay timing of the relays was later determined to be unacceptable, and they were replaced with solid state relays.
    • Arduino - Arduino was the microcontroller used to implement the control system.
    • Python - Python was used to implement the PC user interface.
  • Conferences
    • Travel planning - A flight was booked which included a layover, which seemed innocuous, but resulted in nearly missing the conference presentation after weather grounded flights at the layover airport.
    • Presenting - The presentation given at the finals for the 2016 Student Manufacturing Design Competition was my first experience giving presentations to a technical audience at a conference. One important lesson learned was to ensure sufficient coffee consumption before the presentation, inversely proportional to the amount of sleep in the preceding night. Another lesson learned was to have a timer running on a chair within view of the presentation area to ensure that an appropriate pace was maintained throughout the presentation.
